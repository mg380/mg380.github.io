About

I am a Senior Data Scientist with over 6 years of experience across AI systems, statistical modelling, and large-scale scientific computing. I've spent my career delivering production-ready ML systems in sectors ranging from healthcare and fintech to renewable energy.

Currently, I'm working as a Freelance Data Scientist, specializing in agentic AI architectures and anomaly detection. I'm passionate about building intelligent systems that don't just work in a lab, but drive measurable, real-world impact.

With a Ph.D. in Particle Physics and a background as a former member of the ATLAS experiment at CERN, I bring a rigorous analytical approach to complex data challenges. Whether it's optimizing renewable energy simulations or building financial forensics platforms, I thrive on turning messy data into actionable insights.

My Approach

I bridge the gap between high-energy physics rigor and agile AI deployment. My philosophy is rooted in "Data Integrity First"—ensuring the foundation is solid before building complex models. I specialize in taking research-grade concepts and transforming them into robust, production-ready systems that scale.
